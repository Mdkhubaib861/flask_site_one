Interstellar Movie

Interstellar is a science fiction movie released in 2014 and directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie focuses on space travel, black holes, and time dilation. Earth is slowly becoming uninhabitable. Astronauts travel through a wormhole to find a new home. The film mixes real science with emotional storytelling. It is praised for its visuals, music, and realism.

Black Holes & Time

A black hole has extremely strong gravity. Even light cannot escape from it. Time slows down near a black hole due to gravity. Interstellar showed a realistic black hole called Gargantua. Scientists helped design its appearance. Minutes near the black hole equal years on Earth. This concept is based on Einstein’s theory of relativity.

The Mystery of Space

Space is vast, silent, and full of mysteries. Humans have explored only a small part of the universe. Space travel requires advanced technology and courage. Scientists search for planets that may support life. Interstellar shows how small humans are in the cosmos. The movie connects science with hope and survival. It reminds us that curiosity drives human progress.
